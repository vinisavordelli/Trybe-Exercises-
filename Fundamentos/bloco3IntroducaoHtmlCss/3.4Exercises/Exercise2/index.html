One Punch Man is a Shrimp

When you think of someone strong you think of whom? Eddie Hall?
And someone who could throw a punch? Muhammad Ali? Mike Tyson?
If the Mantis Shrimp was the size of a human being not only would he beat all those 3 without breaking a sweat but he also would be the bringer of armageddon.
This dude punches so fast and so strong water around its punch boils, reaching 4400 celsius degrees if we could accelerate our arms at 1/10th of the speed this shrimp can we would be able to launch small objects into orbit.
And you think it stops there? Its punch is so strong that sends a shockwave that gets so hot that it emits light for a brief moment! If you still don't get how absurd that is, imagine if it was in a boxing fight, it would punch you, and even if it missed it would kill you.
